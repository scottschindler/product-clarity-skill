# ProductClarity Question Bank

Use these questions to sharpen vague product ideas.

Do not ask all questions. Pick the highest-leverage ones.

## User

1. Who is the first specific user?
2. Is this for consumers, prosumers, businesses, internal teams, creators, students, or operators?
3. What kind of person would feel this pain weekly?
4. Who would be embarrassed, annoyed, or frustrated enough to try this?

## Problem

5. What painful moment triggers the user to look for this?
6. What does the user currently do instead?
7. What is broken, slow, annoying, expensive, or emotionally painful?
8. Is this a vitamin, painkiller, or workflow unlock?

## Outcome

9. What does the user get within the first 5 minutes?
10. What does success look like after one use?
11. What does success look like after one month?
12. What would make the user say, “I need this again”?

## Wedge

13. What is the narrowest useful version?
14. What is the first use case where the product can be 10x better?
15. What audience is easiest to reach first?
16. What existing behavior can this attach to?

## MVP

17. What is the smallest version that still feels magical?
18. What can be faked manually at first?
19. What should not be built in v1?
20. What is the one feature the product cannot work without?

## Differentiation

21. Why is this not just a ChatGPT wrapper?
22. What proprietary workflow, taste, data, or structure makes it better?
23. What does the product know that a generic assistant does not?
24. What unique output does it produce?

## Monetization

25. Who pays?
26. Why would they pay now?
27. Is pricing usage-based, subscription, one-time, or service-assisted?
28. What value metric does price map to?

## Growth

29. What output is naturally shareable?
30. Where does the first audience already spend time?
31. What is the viral loop?
32. What would someone post after using this?

## Risk

33. What assumption could kill the product?
34. What needs to be true for this to work?
35. What can be tested in 48 hours?
36. What would prove users do not care?
